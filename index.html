Here's some information to learn about the voiting process.

  • You must be 16 to register to vote
  • You must be 18 to vote in an upcoming election
  • If you are not 18, you will not recieve a ballot to vote in the election
  • You must be a U.S. citizen
  • You can NOT have a felony on your record
  • You must have a DMV number to register online
  • If you do not have a DMV number, you must register in person
